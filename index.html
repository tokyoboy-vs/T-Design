Product Description

Mod. 610

The original.

The Mod. 610 is a simple and practical wallet, made from 100% Swiss wood and using the finest vegetable-tanned Italian leather. The wallet is available in walnut or oak wood.

The wallet has an internal pocket to hold cards and notes. The Mod. 610 is completely handmade and comes with a serial number and certificate of authenticity.

Sizes:

Width: 10,5cm

Height: 6,5cm

Thickness: 1,5cm
